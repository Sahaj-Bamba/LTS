Remember that visit to the superstore? That visit when, for a change, you didn't drag stuff into your cart mindlessly. And finally got your eyes set on a glass jar, not because they said it's unbreakable but because it was not really a jar. It was neither round nor square, not too long yet not too small, neither too heavy nor too light. And you certainly didn't pick it because it was air-tight. You just picked it simply for the way it looked. It said,"I am not a bottle, not a jar, not a canister and definitely not a box." But soon after you brought it home, you realised that it doesn't fit in your fridge, it looks odd among other jars on the shelf, can no way adjust with the plastic boxes in your cabinet. Basically, it doesn't fit in. So it finally finds some place in the middle of your dining table. Let's not discuss what has it been doing there. But the fact is, it's still there. And now you have started to like it there. Not because it fits in but because it stands out. We are all that odd size jar. The only difference is we are happy with the way we are when others are still trying to fit in. And which is why we become what you want us to be. You may call us an event agency, an advertising start-up or just a bunch of creative people but the fact is we will always be that odd size jar.
And which is exactly why you still want us right there in the middle of your dining table.
